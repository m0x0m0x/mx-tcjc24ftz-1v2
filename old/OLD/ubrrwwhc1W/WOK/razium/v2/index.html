How it works

Three simples steps for galactic domination

01

The new Spaceship Designer app is set to revolutionize intergalactic spaceship construction

This powerful desktop application allows users to easily design spaceships of any size and configuration, outfitting them with the latest in military weaponry technology for domination of the galaxy.

02

The intuitive drag-and-drop interface makes designing even the most complex battlecruisers simple

Users can select from pre-made templates or build custom spacecraft from the ground up. State-of-the-art rendering shows ships from any angle with detailed system views. With a few clicks, massive laser cannons, plasma ray emitters, and quantum torpedo launchers can be added to hardpoints on the ship's exterior. The app features an extensive database of devastating weapons to choose from.

03

Once designed, the app generates a complete set of engineering schematics and material/cost lists for construction

With Spaceship Designer, intergalactic governments and private militaries will be able to customize and produce advanced warships to assert their dominance throughout the cosmos. The easy-to-use interface makes vessel design accessible to all, from military experts to novice warship enthusiasts. This app represents a huge leap forward in spaceship construction technology and will rapidly accelerate the arms race across the galaxy.
